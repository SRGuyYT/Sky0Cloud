Sky0Cloud Web App
Optimized for iPhone & iPad – add to Home Screen to continue

To get the best experience on Apple devices, Sky0Cloud runs as a Home Screen web app with notifications enabled.

Steps:
  1. Tap the Share icon in Safari.
  2. Choose Add to Home Screen and confirm.
  3. Open Sky0Cloud from your Home Screen icon.
Once added, tap Send Code. We’ll show a one-time code as a “notification” here. Enter it to unlock this device for future access.
